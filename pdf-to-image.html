Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between JPG and PNG? +
JPG produces smaller file sizes and is great for photographs and most documents. PNG is lossless (no quality loss) and is best for documents with sharp text, diagrams, or transparent elements.
What does the scale setting do? +
Scale controls the resolution of the output image. 1x gives 72 DPI (screen quality), 2x gives 144 DPI (good for most uses), and 3x gives 216 DPI (print quality). Higher scale means larger file sizes.
Are my files uploaded to a server? +
No. Everything runs in your browser using PDF.js. Your files never leave your device.
Can I convert just one page? +
After conversion, each page has its own individual Download button. You can download just the pages you need.

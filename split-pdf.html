Frequently Asked Questions

Yes! Use "Select Pages" mode to click individual pages, or use "By Range" and type specific page numbers like "1, 3, 5".
Are my files private? ▾
Completely. Your PDF is processed in your browser and never uploaded to any server.
Is there a page limit? ▾
No limits. You can split PDFs with any number of pages for free.

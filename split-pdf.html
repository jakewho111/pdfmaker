Why Use PDF Maker to Split PDFs?

Splitting a PDF is one of the most common tasks for students, office workers, and developers. Whether you need to extract specific chapters from a report, separate invoices from a batch file, or pull out individual pages to share, PDF Maker's PDF splitter handles it all. With four split modes — by range, page selection, every N pages, and extract all — you have complete control. Best of all, your PDF never leaves your device, keeping sensitive documents completely private.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I extract just specific pages? ▾
Yes! Use "Select Pages" mode to click individual pages you want, or use "By Range" and type specific page numbers like "1, 3, 5" to extract those exact pages.
How do I split a PDF into separate pages? ▾
Select "All Pages" mode and click Split. Every page of your PDF will be extracted into its own separate PDF file and downloaded.
Is there a page limit? ▾
No limits. You can split PDFs of any size with any number of pages. All processing happens in your browser.
Are my files private? ▾
Completely. Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser and is never uploaded to any server.

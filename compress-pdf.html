Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF compressor really free? ▾
Yes, completely free. No account needed, no watermarks, no daily limits. PDF Maker is free forever.
Is my PDF file safe? ▾
Absolutely. Your file is processed entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. It is never uploaded to any server, so your documents stay 100% private on your device.
How much can I reduce my PDF size? ▾
Results vary depending on the content of your PDF. PDFs with many embedded images typically compress more (up to 70%) while text-heavy PDFs compress less. On average, expect 20–50% reduction.
Will compression reduce the quality? ▾
The Low compression setting preserves near-original quality. Medium is ideal for most uses. High compression reduces quality slightly but is fine for reading on screen or sharing digitally.
What is the maximum file size I can compress? ▾
There is no server-side limit since everything runs in your browser. Very large files (200MB+) may be slower depending on your device's processing speed.

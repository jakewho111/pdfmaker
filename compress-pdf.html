Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF compressor really free? ▾
Yes, completely free. No account needed, no watermarks, no daily limits.
Is my PDF file safe? ▾
Your file is processed entirely inside your browser. It is never uploaded to any server.
How much can I reduce my PDF size? ▾
Results vary. PDFs with many embedded images compress more (up to 70%) while text-heavy PDFs compress less. On average expect 20–50% reduction.
Will compression reduce the quality? ▾
Low compression preserves near-original quality. Medium is ideal for most uses. High reduces quality slightly but is fine for sharing digitally.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported? +
PDF Maker supports JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, and GIF image formats.
Do the page size and orientation settings actually work? +
Yes! Each setting is applied before conversion. A4 portrait gives you 595×842pt pages, landscape gives 842×595pt. Fit to Image creates pages exactly sized to each image. Margins add whitespace around each image.
Can I convert multiple images to one PDF? +
Yes — each image becomes one page in the output PDF. Use the "Add More" button to keep adding images.
Are my images uploaded to a server? +
No. Everything runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your images never leave your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported? ▾
PDF Maker supports JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, and GIF image formats.
Can I convert multiple images to one PDF? ▾
Yes! Add as many images as you need. Each image becomes one page in the PDF. You can also add more images after your initial selection.
Are my images uploaded to a server? ▾
No. Everything runs in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
Will the PDF have watermarks? ▾
No watermarks ever. PDF Maker is completely free with no branding added to your files.

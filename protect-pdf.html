Frequently Asked Questions

How strong is the encryption? ▾
PDF Maker uses AES-256 encryption, the same standard used by banks and governments. It is extremely secure as long as you use a strong password.
Can I unlock a PDF without the password? ▾
No. This tool only removes password protection when you provide the correct password. It cannot crack, guess, or bypass unknown passwords. This is by design — to keep the tool ethical and legal.
Is my PDF uploaded to a server? ▾
No. Everything happens in your browser. Your file and password are never sent to any server. This makes it safe for confidential documents.
What do the permission settings do? ▾
Even after entering the correct password to open the PDF, you can restrict what users can do — like prevent printing, copying text, or editing the document. These are PDF-standard permissions supported by Adobe Reader and most PDF viewers.

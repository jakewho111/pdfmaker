Frequently Asked Questions

How strong is the encryption? ▾
PDF Maker uses AES-256 encryption — the same standard used by banks and governments. It is extremely secure when combined with a strong password.
Can I unlock a PDF without the password? ▾
No. This tool only removes password protection when you provide the correct password. It cannot crack, guess, or bypass unknown passwords.
Is my PDF uploaded to a server? ▾
No. Everything happens in your browser. Your file and password are never sent to any server.
What do the permission settings do? ▾
You can restrict what users can do even after opening the PDF — like prevent printing, copying text, or editing. These are standard PDF permissions supported by Adobe Reader and most PDF viewers.

About PDF Maker

A free all-in-one PDF toolkit that respects your privacy. No uploads, no watermarks, no limits — ever.

Our Mission

PDF Maker was built out of frustration with existing PDF tools. Every free tool either limits you to 2 tasks per day, slaps a watermark on your documents, requires you to upload sensitive files to a remote server, or eventually asks you to pay.

We believe basic PDF tools should be free, unlimited, and private — always. That's why every single tool on PDF Maker runs entirely inside your browser. Your files never leave your device. Not even for a second.

Our revenue comes from non-intrusive Google AdSense advertising, which lets us keep the tools completely free for everyone without compromising your privacy or your documents.

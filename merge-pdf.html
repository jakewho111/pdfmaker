Why Use PDF Maker to Merge PDFs?

Combining multiple PDF documents into a single file is one of the most common PDF tasks — whether you're merging invoices, combining chapters of a report, or joining scanned pages. PDF Maker's free PDF merger works entirely in your browser, meaning your documents are never uploaded to any server. You can merge as many PDFs as you need with no daily limits and no watermarks added to the output. The drag-and-drop reordering lets you set the exact page order before merging.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many PDFs can I merge at once? ▾
There is no limit. You can merge as many PDF files as you need. Very large batches may take longer depending on your device speed.
Is my data safe? ▾
Yes. All merging happens in your browser. Your PDF files are never sent to any server. They stay completely private on your device.
Can I reorder the files before merging? ▾
Yes! Simply drag and drop the file cards to set the order you want. The merged PDF will follow that order exactly.
Will the merged PDF have watermarks? ▾
No watermarks, ever. PDF Maker is completely free with no branding added to your output files.

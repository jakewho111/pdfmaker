Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The short version: PDF Maker processes all files entirely in your browser. Your documents are never uploaded to our servers. We don't store, read, or have access to any files you use with our tools.

1. File Processing

All PDF tools on PDF Maker operate entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. When you upload a file to any of our tools, that file is loaded into your browser's memory and processed locally on your device. The file is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

This means:

  • Your files remain on your device at all times
  • We have no technical ability to access your documents
  • No file content is ever stored, logged, or analyzed by us
  • Closing your browser tab immediately removes all processed data from memory

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal anonymous data to understand how our site is used:

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics such as page views, time on site, and which tools are most used. This data cannot identify you personally.
  • Cookies: Google Analytics sets cookies to distinguish unique visitors. These are anonymous session cookies.

3. Google AdSense

PDF Maker displays advertisements provided by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. This is governed by Google's own privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of personalized ads at google.com/settings/ads.

4. No Account Required

PDF Maker does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifying information.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

6. Children's Privacy

PDF Maker does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our About page.
